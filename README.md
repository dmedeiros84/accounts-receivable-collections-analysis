# Accounts Receivable & Collections Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes accounts receivable data for a fictional B2B services company, Northwind Services Inc. The goal is to improve visibility into overdue invoices, identify high-risk customers, and support better collections prioritization.

The finance team was facing challenges with manual tracking, limited visibility into overdue balances, and difficulty identifying which accounts required immediate follow-up.

## Business Problem

The company lacked a structured way to monitor overdue invoices and prioritize collection activities. This created risks related to cash flow delays, manual work, financial exposure, and low visibility into customer payment behavior.

## Project Objectives

- Improve visibility into outstanding and overdue balances
- Identify high-risk customers and aging patterns
- Support collection prioritization
- Provide leadership with clear AR performance indicators
- Reduce manual analysis through an interactive dashboard

## Dataset

The dataset contains simulated accounts receivable invoice data, including customer, region, industry, invoice date, due date, payment status, outstanding amount, aging bucket, and collection priority.

## Key KPIs

- Total Invoiced Amount
- Total Paid Amount
- Outstanding Amount
- Overdue Amount
- Collection Rate
- Overdue %
- Number of Overdue Invoices
- Average Days Late
- High Priority Amount

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. AR Overview

This page provides a high-level view of accounts receivable performance, including total invoiced amount, outstanding balance, overdue amount, collection rate, overdue percentage, aging buckets, top overdue customers, and overdue exposure by region.

![AR Overview](images/dashboard_screenshot.png)

### 2. Collections Insights

This page focuses on operational and risk insights, including high-risk exposure, overdue trends, collection priority, industry exposure, and invoice-level details for follow-up actions.

## Key Insights

- A significant portion of overdue balances is concentrated in specific customers and industries.
- High-risk accounts require immediate collection actions.
- Aging buckets above 60 days represent increased financial exposure.
- Regional differences suggest opportunities for better monitoring and follow-up.
- Collection priority can help the finance team focus on the most urgent accounts first.

## Recommendations

- Prioritize collections based on aging bucket, customer risk, and outstanding amount.
- Create automated alerts for invoices overdue by more than 60 days.
- Review payment terms for recurring high-risk customers.
- Use monthly AR dashboards to improve leadership visibility.
- Track collection performance regularly to reduce overdue exposure.
